Lee (Lesley Choi)

Korean Lawyer • Strategic Mind • Friend of David

Biography

Lee, whose full name is Lesley Choi, is widely considered the intellectual powerhouse of the friend group. A Korean lawyer with a sharp mind and strategic thinking, he once shared an apartment with David in New York during their younger years.

Lee was known for dating fashion models during his bachelor years and eventually married one. His life later took a meaningful turn when he moved to the peaceful town of Heiwa with his friends.

In Heiwa, Lee opened his own law firm where he works passionately to help people with their legal struggles. More importantly to him, he sees his work as an opportunity to bring hope and speak about the message of Jesus Christ to those who feel lost.

Despite his serious career, Lee deeply values his lifelong friendships with David, Debby, Lisa, and Mike.

Fun Facts

  • Known as the "brains" of the group.
  • Extremely analytical and strategic.
  • Runs a successful law firm in Heiwa.
  • Uses his work to encourage people spiritually.
