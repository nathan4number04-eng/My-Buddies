Lisa

Portuguese – Shop Owner – David's Best Friend

Biography

Lisa is a Portuguese woman and the best friend of David. She grew up in the same small town with David and his older sister Deborah (whom everyone called Debby). A few years ago, she moved to Heiwa and reunited with her American friends David, Debby, Mike, and Lee.

Lisa now owns a Christian bookstore in Heiwa, where she hopes to spread the word of Christ through the books she sells. She is known for her kind heart, strong faith, and dedication to her friends.

Fun Facts

  • Loves reading and sharing Christian books.
  • Deeply values her friendships and faith.
  • Has a warm and welcoming personality.
