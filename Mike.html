Mike (Michael)

Humble Heir • Cab Driver • Friend to Everyone

Biography

Mike is the first-born son of an African billionaire and the heir to an enormous fortune. Despite this incredible wealth, Mike is widely known among his friends as the most humble member of the group.

Having grown up behind the guarded walls of a massive mansion, Mike longed to understand everyday life and meet ordinary people. To do this, he moved to New York and began working as a cab driver, wanting genuine conversations with the people he drove across the city.

It was in New York where Mike met David, Debby, Lisa, and Lee. The five quickly became inseparable friends.

Later, Mike moved with them to the peaceful town of Heiwa in Japan. Because few people there know about his family fortune, Mike continues to live simply and still works as a cab driver, sometimes driving Uber, enjoying the chance to talk with people from all walks of life.

Mike often uses these everyday encounters to share encouragement and speak about his faith in Jesus Christ with his passengers.

Mike is married to a Jewish YouTuber whom he met during his years in New York.

Fun Facts

  • Heir to a massive African fortune.
  • Works as a cab driver by choice.
  • Met the friend group while living in New York.
  • Loves having deep conversations with passengers.
  • Uses everyday life to spread the message of faith.
