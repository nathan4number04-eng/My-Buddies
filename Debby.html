Deborah (Debby)

Wise Sister • Loyal Friend • Woman of Faith

Biography

Deborah, known to her friends as Debby, is David’s older sister and one of the central members of the group of friends.

She grew up in the same small town as David and Lisa and has always been known for her wisdom, calm personality, and caring nature.

Debby played an important role in the lives of the group, often helping settle disagreements and offering thoughtful advice when life became difficult.

After the group’s years in New York, Debby eventually moved to the peaceful town of Heiwa in Japan, where she reunited with David, Lisa, Mike, and Lee.

Her strong Christian faith guides her life, and she often encourages her friends to stay hopeful and trust in God even during challenging times.

Debby lives in Heiwa with her son Winston and remains one of the emotional anchors of the group.

Fun Facts
